# Empathy Driven Water Purifier for Rural Homes

## Overview

* Low-cost water purifier designed for rural households
* Focuses on user needs, accessibility, and ease of use
* Provides safe and clean drinking water

## Features

* Simple and user-friendly design
* No electricity or low power requirement
* Uses natural filtration methods
* Affordable and easy to maintain

## Process

* Collect raw water
* Pass through filtration layers (sand, charcoal, etc.)
* Remove impurities and contaminants
* Store clean water for use

## Advantages

* Improves health and hygiene
* Affordable for rural communities
* Easy to use and maintain
* Reduces waterborne diseases

## Limitations

* Limited purification compared to advanced systems
* Needs regular cleaning and maintenance

## Future Scope

* Add advanced filtration technology
* Improve durability
* Scale for wider rural adoption
